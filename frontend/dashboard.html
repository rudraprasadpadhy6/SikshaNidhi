Welcome to SikshaNidhi!

SikshaNidhi is an AI-powered scholarship & financial opportunities platform built to help students discover, track, and apply for scholarships and grants that match their unique profile. This guide walks you through every feature so you can hit the ground running.

Quick Start in 3 Steps

  • Step 1 — Dashboard: Your command centre. See live scholarships and deadline alerts at a glance.
  • Step 2 — Choose a Portal: Click UniScholar.AI for academic scholarships, or UniCapital.AI for financial aid & grants.
  • Step 3 — Chat with PixaBot: Our AI assistant is always available at the bottom of the screen to guide you through anything.
You can open this guide anytime by clicking the ? button next to the notification bell at the top of the page.

Notification Bell

The bell icon shows scholarships with upcoming deadlines (closing within 7 days). A red pulsing dot appears when urgent alerts exist. Click the bell to expand the list — each entry includes the official link and required documents.

Live Scholarships Sidebar

The left panel displays a real-time list of active scholarships fetched from our database. Each card shows the name, status, and a Docs Required link. Click any card to open the official scholarship page in a new tab.

Portal Buttons

  • UniScholar.AI — AI-powered scholarship matching based on your academic profile.
  • UniCapital.AI — Capital & financial aid portal for grants, loans, and funding.

User Profile (Top-Right)

Your display name appears in the top-right corner, loaded from your saved session so it always shows the name you registered with.

The motivational quote at the top of the dashboard cycles automatically every few seconds!

What is UniScholar.AI?

An intelligent scholarship-matching engine. It analyses your academic profile, financial background, and personal details to recommend the most suitable scholarships from a curated national & international database.

How to Use UniScholar.AI

  • Click UniScholar.AI on the dashboard.
  • Fill in your details — course, marks/GPA, state, category, family income, etc.
  • Click Find Scholarships — the AI matches your profile instantly.
  • Browse personalised results with eligibility, amount, deadline & required docs.
  • Click any result to visit the official portal and begin your application.

Documents Checklist

Each scholarship card includes a Docs Required button. Click it to see the exact list of documents you need to prepare — mark sheets, income certificates, Aadhaar details, etc.

Keep your documents (mark sheets, income proof, Aadhaar, bank passbook) ready as PDFs to speed up the application process.

What is UniCapital.AI?

The financial intelligence portal. Discover capital funding, educational loans, government grants, and investment opportunities that go beyond traditional scholarships — tailored to your profile.

How to Use UniCapital.AI

  • Click UniCapital.AI on the dashboard.
  • Enter your financial requirements, academic level, and goals.
  • The AI analyses available loan schemes, grants, and capital opportunities.
  • Results show interest rates, repayment terms, eligibility, and official links.

Types of Opportunities

  • Education Loans — subsidised and unsubsidised bank loans for higher education.
  • Government Grants — state and central government funding programs.
  • Startup & Skill Capital — funds for skill development and entrepreneurship.
Compare multiple capital options side by side to choose the one with the best terms for your situation.

What is PixaBot?

PixaBot is your personal AI assistant powered by Groq. It can answer any question about scholarships, financial aid, eligibility, and how to use the platform. Just type or speak naturally — it understands you!

How to Open & Use PixaBot

  • Find the circular PixaBot avatar floating at the bottom of the screen.
  • Click it to open the chat window.
  • Type your question and press Send, or use the microphone button for voice input.
  • You can drag the PixaBot bubble anywhere on screen.
  • Click outside the chat or the bubble again to close it.

Voice Input

Click the microphone icon inside the chat box, allow microphone access when prompted, and speak your question. PixaBot will automatically transcribe and respond to your voice message.

Try asking: "What scholarships am I eligible for as a B.Tech student with 80% marks?" — PixaBot will walk you through the next steps!

How to Submit Feedback

  • Click the Feedback button at the bottom-right corner of any page.
  • Rate your experience using the star rating (1–5 stars).
  • Select a category: General Feedback, Bug Report, Feature Suggestion, or Compliment.
  • Write your message (up to 500 characters) and click Send Feedback.

Reporting a Bug

Select Bug Report as the category, describe what happened in detail, and submit. Our team reviews all reports privately and will address issues promptly.

Your feedback is completely private and only visible to the SikshaNidhi admin team. We read every single one!
